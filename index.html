Latest Research

2019 April A transiting exocomet detected in broadband light by TESS in the Beta Pictoris system is accepted for publication in Astronomy and Astropysics Letters. We model a dip in the light curve seen towards the young star Beta Pictoris as being caused by a cometary tail, confirming a 20 year old prediction.
