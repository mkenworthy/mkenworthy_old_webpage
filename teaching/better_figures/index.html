How to make better figures

Theory and practice in improving scientific diagrams

A two day course presented in March 2016 at Leiden Observatory

Lecturers: Magnus Persson, Cristobal Sifon, Nadia Murillo, Rafa Monterde Alonso, Matthew Kenworthy

Consultation: Anna Miotello, Jarle Brinchmann

Over two afternoon sessions we presented a series of talks with the theme of how to improve the readability and clarity of your scientific plots and figures in your papers. Python notebooks are used to demonstrate and try out the principles for the students. The lecture notes and Python notebooks are presented below.<
